Projects

IGNNITION

Resulting from my work in my Bachelor Thesis, my research lab (Barcelona Neural Networking Center) received a European grand to further develop our project, IGNNITION.

This is a framework that provides a code-free interface that permits researchers and practicioners to implement in a matter of hours (instead of days/weeks) potentially very complex Graph Neural Network models. Concretely, complex PyTorch/Tensorflow implementation is substituted by simple natural language descriptiors flexible enough to implement virtually any GNN.

To date, IGNNITION has received over 19k downloads!

About

My name is David Pujol Perich. I am currently a Ph.D student at Hubpba lab from Universitat de Barcelona, where I study the application of efficient Transformer models to video understanding-based tasks.

I obtained my Bachelors degree in Computer Science at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), which I combined with a study exchange at ETHZ, Switzerland. I also obtained my Masters Degree at UPC in Research and Innovation in Informatics with a strong focus on Advanced Computing. I also combined this program with a 1 year exchange at EPFL, Switzerland, where I undertook both courses on Deep Learning and my Master Thesis.

During this time I also combined my studies with professional experience. First of all, I worked as a Machine Learning Research intern at Barcelona Neural Networking Center during almost 2 years. I then worked at LIONS, EPFL as a Machine Learning Research Assistant during 1 year.

All in all, my research interests lay in the field of Deep Learning in general and their application in fields such as Computer Vision or NLP.

I am always open to collaborations and constructive discussions. So do not hesitate to shoot me a mesage!!
